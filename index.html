Crystal Collector Game!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specifc amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by amtching your total score to a random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above a random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the value of each crystal will change! Have FUN!!!
